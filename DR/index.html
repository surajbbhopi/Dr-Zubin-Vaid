About Doctor

Dr. Zubin Vaid

M.D.(Med), FIDM.(Diabetes)
Consulting Physician & Diabetologist

Trusted Physician. Compassionate Caregiver. Dedicated Healer.

With over 25 years of experience in Internal Medicine and Critical Care, Dr. Zubin Mehernosh Vaid has been helping patients navigate complex health conditions with confidence, clarity, and compassion.

Known for his calm demeanor and meticulous clinical approach, Dr. Vaid believes that every patient deserves personalized attention and evidence-based medical care. Over the years, he has successfully treated thousands of patients with conditions ranging from diabetes and hypertension to infectious diseases, respiratory illnesses, thyroid disorders, and critical care emergencies.

Patients often appreciate not only his medical expertise but also the time he takes to listen, explain, and guide them through their healthcare journey.

Driven by a commitment to lifelong learning and ethical medical practice, Dr. Vaid combines modern medicine with a holistic understanding of patient well-being, helping individuals achieve better health and a better quality of life.

Treating People, Not Just Diseases

Good healthcare begins with understanding.

Dr. Vaid believes that every patient is unique, and so is their health journey. Rather than focusing only on symptoms, he strives to understand the complete picture - medical history, lifestyle, concerns, and long-term health goals.

His approach is built on three simple principles:

Listen Carefully

Every patient deserves to be heard. Understanding concerns is the first step toward accurate diagnosis and effective treatment.

Treat Thoughtfully

Evidence-based medicine, combined with clinical experience and personalized care, forms the foundation of every treatment plan.

Support Continuously

Healthcare doesn't end after a consultation. Dr. Vaid believes in building lasting relationships with patients and guiding them toward long-term wellness.

Waiting time for consultations

We value your time and try our best to have the least waiting for a consultation. However, this may not always be possible because of various factors like the varying time required for each consultation. Hence there can never be an assurance for a maximum waiting period and waiting times could get extremely prolonged especially for extra appointments. Please bear with us for the same.

Reports

There has to be an appointment taken to show the reports either with me or my assistant which will usually be given at the time of asking for the tests. Please note that reports are strictly not to be sent by digital media including WhatsApp, mail or SMS except in very extreme circumstances where previous permission is required to send them by mail only. Again this is in an extreme circumstance with prior permission, not otherwise.

Home Visits and home based sickness/death/will/fitness certificates

In view of opds and multiple hospital visits and emergencies, there is no scope for me to make house visits for any reason at any time. Hence it is prudent to please keep in touch with your family doctor/general practitioner for minor ailments, advice, certificates at home, house visits etc. even if you are regularly following up with me. I can definitely help by coordinating with the doctor telephonically whenever necessary.
